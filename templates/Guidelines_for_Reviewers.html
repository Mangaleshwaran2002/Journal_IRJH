Ethical Guidelines for Reviewers

  • Reviewers are requested not to accept the invitation to review a research work if they do not agree to the ethics policy of the journal
  • Reviewers are requested not to accept the invitation to review if they find the work submitted for review is not relevant to their area of expertise.
  • Reviewers should maintain the confidentiality of their work.
  • Reviewers may approach the editors if they have any queries regarding the journal and its policies.
  • Reviewers should assess a work honestly, rigorously and objectively.
  • The review report should be sent to the editor within 28 days from the receipt of the research work for review.
