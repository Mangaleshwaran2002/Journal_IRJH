INFORMATION FOR AUTHORS

Interdisciplinary Research Journal for Humanities (IRJH)

Our journal, Interdisciplinary Research Journal for Humanities (IRJH) (ISSN: 2249-250X), a Peer Reviewed Journal was started in 2012. It is a biannual journal. We publish authentic and unpublished articles under the branch of humanities for the last ten years. This includes subjects like Tamil, English, Folklore, Social Work and Social Science.

The paper must be submitted through the journal editorial system. The authors must use British English spelling in the manuscript uniformly

Format

  • Font : Times New Roman
  • Font size : 12
  • Space : 1.5
  • Style : MLA 8th Edition
  • Abstract : 50 to 75 words
  • Key words: 5 to 7
  • Full Paper : 2500 to 3000 words
  • Work Cited: Only the works cited in the article should be mentioned

Ethical Guidelines for Authors

  • Authors should ensure that the research paper submitted is original and has not been published elsewhere.
  • All the authors must be represented accurately and clear references are to be given to the work done by others.
  • The complete responsibility is on the authors to reveal the source of all data and third-party material used.
  • Use of any third-party material is to be done after obtaining the written permission from the respective copyright holders. The permission documents should be submitted along with the research work submitted for publication and the publisher or editor cannot be held responsible for the improper use of copyrighted material in the research work at any time. The authors are responsible if any investigation is initiated by the editor or publisher, in case data used in a study is found to be false,insufficient, or misrepresented. Authors should respond to the editor or publisher when source of data, proof of authorship or any such detail is requested.
  • When any ethical or technical issue is found with a published research work, the authors should be ready to provide corrigenda in a timely manner and must report to the editor and publisher in case of publication of an errata or retraction of the published research work.
