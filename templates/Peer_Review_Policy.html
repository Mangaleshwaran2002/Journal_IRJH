Peer Review Policy

Reviewers support the editorial board in making editorial decisions.

The editorial board is responsible for confirming the competence of the reviewers. Contributors will receive notification about the acceptance of his/her manuscript for the reviewing process within two weeks. The editorial board is responsible for ensuring the promptness of responses in the double blind review process. Review is to be conducted objectively and personal criticism of the author should be avoided. The Referee's report should send the reports towards enhancing the quality of the manuscript.
