Interdisciplinary Research Journal for Humanities (IRJH)

Our journal, Interdisciplinary Research Journal for Humanities (IRJH) (ISSN: 2249-250X), a Peer Reviewed Journal was started in 2012. It is a biannual journal. We publish authentic and unpublished articles under the branch of humanities for the last ten years. This includes subjects like Tamil, English, Folklore, Social Work and Social Science.
