Ethical Guidelines for Editors

  • Editors should confirm that the research work submitted for publication adheres to the requirements of the journal.
  • Editors should do objective and confidential review of all original research submissions. The initial quality check should also include an originality check using a plagiarism detection software amongst other appropriate methods.
  • All publication processes should be transparent and any queries from the authors regarding the publication process should be answered in a timely manner.
  • All research work submitted for publication should be subjected to a double blind peer review and the review report should be submitted to the publisher for archival purposes.
  • Editors should ensure that the reviewers submit their reviews in the format given by the publisher and the review is done within a month from the receipt of the article
  • Editors will inform all editorial decision to the publisher.
  • Any article found using false data or has a detrimental impact on society should not be considered for publication.
